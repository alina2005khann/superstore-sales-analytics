## 🔍 Key Insights
- **Technology** = highest profit $144,998
- **Furniture** earns 88% less than Technology
- **40%+ discounts** caused $122K loss
- **No discount** = $320K profit
- **Sep, Nov, Dec** = peak months every year
- **West region** = best at $107K profit
- **Delhi** dominates city sales

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
1. Cap all discounts at **20% maximum**
2. Discontinue **Tables & Bookcases**
3. Maximize marketing in **Sep/Nov/Dec**
4. Focus on **Consumer VIP** retention
5. Investigate **Central region** costs
